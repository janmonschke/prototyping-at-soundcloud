Prototyping @ SoundCloud

The tools jan mentioned above are great for testing simple interactions or animations but however fall short when dealing with data and distribution. A lot of link sharing and doesn't feel real because you don't have your own data - not genuine feedback

Therefore we've built a version of the SoundCloud app solely made for prototyping purposes.

High Fidelity Prototyping

A prototype that does not feel like a prototype.
  • A fully functioning app / site
  • Real production data
  • ‚ÄùSame‚Äù design like the real thing™
  • Users are presented with their real account data
  • ‚ÄùSimpler‚Äù implementation (e.g. performance)

Simple as in: Does not have all performance / load optimisations but is good enough for a user testing session

Why high fidelity prototyping?

  • Immersion
  • Make use of familiarity
  • Fulfill expectations
  • Less guidance / more freedom
  • Results more trustworthy / real

Mention recently-played test where users where presented with static data. Expectation is key in those prototypes and when user's have an emotional connection to your product (e.g. they are collecting things on your platform) it is important to fulfill those emotional expectations.

real data === not-polished data (long usernames, weird artowrk, color schemes) that you just don't design with

Freedom as in users are free to do whatever and are not taken by their hands. You want them to express themselves freely.

Disadvantages

  • More expensive (‚è≤ + üí∞)
  • Works better for existing products
